Welcome to Vizag Steel Plant

Rashtriya Ispat Nigam Limited (RINL), the corporate entity of Visakhapatnam Steel Plant is a Navaratna PSE under the Ministry of Steel. Visakhapatnam Steel Plant fondly called Vizag Steel. It is the first shore based Integrated Steel Plant in the country and is known for its quality products delighting the customers. It is a market leader in long products and it caters to the needs of diverse Industrial sectors. It is the first Steel plant to be certified ISO 9001:2008 (presently 2015), ISO 14001:2004 (presently 2015), OHSAS 18001:2007 and ISO/IEC 27001:2013 Standards. It is also the first PSE to be certified ISO 50001:2011 - Energy Management Systems and has acquired CMMI Level 3 Certification for s/w development. Any kind of Construction Steel, Fabrication Steel and Special Steel is produced from RINL.

Why RINL?

Quality Reassurance

RINL is committed to producing **high-quality, durable, and precision-engineered steel** products that meet international standards. Our products undergo **rigorous quality control checks** at every stage of production to ensure excellence.

Diverse Product Range

We offer a **wide range of steel products**, including **TMT bars, structural steel, wire rods, forgings, and customized steel solutions** for various industrial and construction needs.

Reliable Supply Chain

With **state-of-the-art production facilities and a strong logistics network**, we guarantee **timely deliveries** across India and international markets, ensuring an uninterrupted supply of steel materials.

Eco-Friendly Steel Production

RINL prioritizes **sustainable manufacturing** with eco-friendly processes, minimal waste, and reduced carbon emissions. Our facilities follow **ISO 14001:2015 Environmental Management Standards** to ensure green production.

Trusted by Industries

Our steel is widely used in **infrastructure, railways, automotive, shipbuilding, power plants, and defense sectors**. We have earned the trust of clients across industries with our **reliable and high-strength steel solutions**.

Customer-Centric Approach

We believe in **building long-term relationships** with our customers by offering **customized steel solutions, competitive pricing, and excellent after-sales support**. Customer satisfaction is our priority.
