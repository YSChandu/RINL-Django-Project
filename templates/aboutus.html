About Us

We are India's leading steel producer, committed to quality and sustainability.

Who We Are ?

Welcome to Vizag Steel Plant, a leading steel producer in India. Established in 1982, we are committed to producing high-quality steel products and contributing to the nation's growth.

Our state-of-the-art facilities and dedicated workforce ensure that we meet the highest standards of quality and efficiency.

At Vizag Steel Plant, we believe in sustainable development, environmental protection, and social responsibility.

Our Vision & Mission

Our vision is to be a world-class steel producer known for innovation, sustainability, and excellence.

We strive to contribute to India's industrial growth while ensuring the well-being of our employees and the community.

Why Choose Us?

  • High-quality steel products
  • State-of-the-art technology
  • Eco-friendly production
  • Customer-centric approach
  • Strong research and innovation

Our Achievements

We have been recognized as one of India's top steel producers with numerous industry awards and certifications.

Our steel is used in major infrastructure projects, contributing to the nation's growth.
